About me

I am a passionate Engineering student pursuing a Master's Thesis in Mechanical Engineering with a specialization in Thermal-Fluid Science and HVAC-Energy Systems. Stepping towards my field of interest and specialization, I am currently working on MS Thesis -'Thermal Homeostasis in buildings via e-powered heat pump system'. The project is based on modeling a heat pump system for residential buildings, using 'Grid-Interactive Technology', coupled with the Thermal Energy Storage (TES) unit (water tank) to increase the efficiency of an HVAC system and reducing the energy cost.

I am enthusiastic about designing and building interdisciplinary engineering solutions in a hands-on-environment and look to further my abilities in a field of Energy-Sustainability and Thermal Engineering solutions. My inner drive for excellence and attention to detail is complemented by a sense of humility to stay focused on my goals. I live by the motto "Always be learning" and am excited about what next there is to learn and experience.
